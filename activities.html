Our Events

A list of our most impactful and recent projects designed to build practical and livelihood skills for our TLE students.

Nutri King & Queen 2025

This event is a reminder that true beauty comes from being healthy, confident, and active. It’s the perfect way to end the celebration while recognizing students who promote good nutrition inside and outside the school.

Date: August 1 2025 | Participants: 35 Students | Covered Court Area

Nutribooth Exhibit: “Mas Healthy, Mas Panalo!”

Here, booths will showcase healthy choices and smart habits. It’s a fun way to explore different foods an nutrition ideas hands-on!

Date: August 1 2025 | Participants: 60 Students | Covered Court Area

Nutri Zumba Week

We included this event because stayingg active is just as important as eating right. Plus, dancing together helps students enjoy exercise, bond with friends, and relieve stress.

Date: July 28 to 31 2025 | Participants: 45 Students | Covered Court Area

On-the-Spot Non-Digital and Digital Poster Mmaking Contest

Non-Digital entries at the Covered Court Area and Digital entries again at the JHS ICT Lab. Art is a powerful tool, and through posters, students can show messages about ealth that are easy to understand and inspiring to look at.

Date: July 25 2025 | Participants: 35 Students | JHS ICT Lab

Infomercial Contest

Students will use their ideas and digital skills to spread awareness about eating healthy. Since social media is a big part of our lives, this activity teaches everyone to share helpful information online in a fun and catchy way.

Date: July 23 2025 | Participants: 60 Students | JHS ICT Lab

Nutri Jingle Dance Contest

We chose this event because music and dancing easily catch everyone’s attention and help promote a fun and active lifestyle. It’s the perfect way to hype up students for the whole celebration!

Date: July 22 2025 | Participants: 45 Students | Covered Court Area
