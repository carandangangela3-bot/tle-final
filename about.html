The TLE Organization

Learn about our purpose and our journey in fostering essential life skills for the students of Muntindilaw National High School.

Our Purpose

The Muntindilaw TLE Organization is dedicated to empowering students with essential hands-on, entrepreneurial, and technical skills across Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE) strands, including ICT, Culinary Arts, and Industrial Arts. Its core mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical application, fostering responsible, self-reliant, and career-ready graduates.
