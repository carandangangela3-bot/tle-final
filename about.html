The TLE Organization

Learn about our purpose and our journey in fostering essential life skills for the students of Muntindilaw National High School.

Our Purpose

The Muntindilaw TLE Organization is dedicated to supporting students in their learning under Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE). The organization promotes hands-on learning, creativity, collaboration, and responsibility among students. Through different school activities, projects, and programs, the organization encourages students to apply what they learn in class to real-life situations.

Our History

The TLE Organization of Muntindilaw National High School was established to bring together students who are interested in practical learning and skill development. Over time, the organization has helped support various school activities and events related to Technology and Livelihood Education. As the school continues to grow, we remain committed to encouraging student participation and contribution.
